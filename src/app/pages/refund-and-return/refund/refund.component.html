Return and Refund Policy

1. Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may be eligible for a return, replacement, or refund in accordance with this policy.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To qualify for a return or refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • Returns must be requested within 30 days from the delivery date.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards or vouchers
  • Products marked as final sale
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

4. Return Process

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support at support@yourelectronicshop.com within 30 days of delivery.
  2. Provide your order ID, receipt number, and reason for return.
  3. Our support team will provide instructions for returning the item.
  4. Ship the item to the address provided by our support team. Retain the tracking number.

5. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to your original payment method.
  • Processing time may take up to 7 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

6. Replacements & Exchanges

We can replace items if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received your refund after 7 business days, please:

  • Check your bank account or payment method.
  • Contact your credit card company, as it may take additional time to post.
  • Reach out to our customer support if the refund is still missing.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If your product arrives damaged or defective, contact us immediately. Include:

  • Order ID and receipt number
  • Photos showing the damage or defect

We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

9. Shipping Returns

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

10. Contact Us

For questions or assistance regarding returns and refunds, contact us at:

  • Email: support@yourelectronicshop.com
  • Phone: +1 800 789 0000

Return & Refund Policy

11. Partial Refunds

Certain circumstances may qualify for partial refunds, such as:

  • Item not in its original condition, damaged, or missing parts.
  • Item returned after the 30-day window (at our discretion).
  • Customized products that can only be partially refunded.

12. Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled before shipment. Once the order is shipped, it is subject to our return policy.

13. Exchanges

If you need to exchange a product for the same item in a different size, color, or model:

  • Contact support within 30 days of delivery.
  • Ensure the product is unused and in original packaging.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the exchange is due to our error.

14. International Returns

International customers may return items, but are responsible for all shipping fees. Customs duties, taxes, and import fees are non-refundable.

15. Late or Missing Returns

If we do not receive your returned item within 14 business days, your refund may be delayed or denied. Always use a trackable shipping method.

16. Restocking Fees

A restocking fee may apply for certain items returned in non-original condition or without original packaging. The fee will be deducted from your refund.

17. Refund Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 7 business days after we receive the returned item. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

18. Non-Returnable Situations

Refunds or returns may be denied in the following cases:

  • Items damaged due to misuse, accidents, or improper handling.
  • Products missing serial numbers or original packaging.
  • Items purchased from third-party sellers or marketplace partners.

19. Legal Rights

This return and refund policy does not affect your statutory rights under applicable law. Customers retain all rights to remedies for defective or non-conforming products.

20. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time without prior notice. Updated policies will be posted on our website. Please review this page periodically.
