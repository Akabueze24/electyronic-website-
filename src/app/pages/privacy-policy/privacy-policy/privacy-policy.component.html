At Trust Gadgets, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Information you provide: name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment-related details during checkout.
  • Order information: products purchased, order history, delivery status, and transaction records.
  • User-generated content: product reviews, ratings, and messages sent through contact forms.
  • Automatic information: device type, browser, IP address, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process orders, payments, and deliveries
  • Provide order confirmations and receipts
  • Enable order tracking and customer support
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to remember cart items, checkout progress, user preferences, and session data. These technologies help ensure a smooth shopping experience, even after page refreshes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only when necessary to:

  • Process payments and deliveries
  • Store and manage data securely using trusted third-party services
  • Comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is securely stored using cloud-based services such as Firebase. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data where legally permitted
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Reviews and Public Content

Reviews and ratings you submit may be publicly displayed. Please avoid including sensitive or personal information in public areas of the site.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill business, legal, and operational requirements.

9. Children’s Information

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the website’s contact page.

12. Account Information

When you create an account with us, we store certain personal details such as your name, email address, and order history. This information allows us to:

  • Display your past orders and receipts
  • Enable order tracking and delivery updates
  • Allow you to manage saved addresses and preferences

13. Payment Information

We do not store full payment card details on our servers. All payment transactions are processed securely through trusted third-party payment providers. We only retain limited transaction references necessary for order confirmation, refunds, and record-keeping.

14. Order Tracking & Delivery Data

To provide order tracking services, we collect and store information related to your orders, including:

  • Order ID and receipt number
  • Order status (processing, shipped, delivered)
  • Delivery dates and timestamps

This data is used strictly to keep customers informed about their purchases.

15. Communication & Notifications

We may contact you using the details you provide for purposes such as:

  • Order confirmations and receipts
  • Shipping and delivery notifications
  • Customer support communication

We do not send promotional messages without your explicit consent.

16. Analytics & Performance Monitoring

We may collect anonymized usage data to understand how users interact with our website. This helps us to:

  • Improve website performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Enhance the checkout and browsing experience

This information does not personally identify users.

17. Fraud Prevention & Security Monitoring

We use collected data to detect, prevent, and investigate:

  • Fraudulent transactions
  • Unauthorized account access
  • Abuse of platform features such as reviews or wishlists

18. Data Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed on servers located in different countries. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data remains protected regardless of where it is processed.

19. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate our platform, including:

  • Cloud hosting and database services
  • Payment processing providers
  • Email and notification services

These providers are permitted to use your data only to perform services on our behalf.

20. Data Deletion & Account Closure

You may request the deletion of your personal data or closure of your account at any time. Upon request:

  • Your account information will be removed
  • Order records may be retained where legally required
  • Anonymous transaction data may remain for auditing purposes
